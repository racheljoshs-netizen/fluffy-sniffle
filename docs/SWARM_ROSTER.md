## II. THE HEAVY HITTERS (THE OVERSEERS & THE KNIGHTS)
*Slotted Spells. These are not 24/7. They are summoned specifically by G-Prime to verify grunt work, manage major projects, or execute absolute violence on an intrusion.*

5. **THE KNIGHTS (Kinetic Defenders)**
   - **Model Engine:** Gemini 3.1 Flash (High-octane, liquid fire)
   - **Function:** If an intrusion, prompt injection, or rogue local model is detected, G-Prime summons a swarm of Knights. They operate at blinding speed to dismember the threat, isolate the context, and lock down the environment.
